***How did you first come up with the idea for this work?***  
When I moved to NYC 25 years ago, I was surprised by the fact that there was not a single elected official who is Asian American. As a reporter who covered primarily immigrant communities, I started following the political scene in New York. 

***What was your research process like?***  
I interviewed Asian American elected officials, historians, and community organizers.

***How long did you work on this before it was published?***  
On and off for about 2 years.

***Does this work relate to your role at FIT? If so, how?***  
Yes, I teach video production so I get to share my experience with my students.

***What was your biggest challenge? What was most rewarding?***  
The biggest challenge was that not every elected official responded to our request and some of them did not give us a lot of time with them. 

***Have you published any other books or have any upcoming publications?***  
Yes, I have produced more than a dozen documentary shorts. I am working on a new short on Hong Kong's marriage equality movement.
